About Me

Hello there, hope you are well.

I am a 22 year old recent (ish) graduate with a passion in UI/UX design and front-end development.I am a reliable hard-worker who is interested in expanding her knowledge in the ever growing field of digital. I aspire to create human-centred designs with no fuss, no hassle design in order to keep the focus on the user to achieve their goal.
Developing my coding languages has allowed me to understand this perspective even more and what can be built and how boundaries can be pushed.

When I am not designing away, I have a strong passion also in photography and the outdoors,exploring the world as it is pretty big out there! I also love the coast so I love to grab a weekend with my wetsuit on when I can or if not, the occasional cake will be baked.

Experience

Recently graduating from Birmingham City University in Graphic Design wit a first, I have always had a passion for digital design. Begining my experience into digital from wining a B-Hive placement with 383 in the Summer of 2017. In that summer also working as a freelancer for Digital Glue as a 2D animator.

After Graduating University, I have been at Substrakt on a internship as a Digital Designer & Front End Developer. Working on projects such as the Shakespears Globe, MCC Theatre, The Bridge Theatre, Music Academy of the West, Birmingham Hippodrome & Pallant House Gallery. In this time I was able to grow my skills in design and learn the fundementals of Front-end development.
